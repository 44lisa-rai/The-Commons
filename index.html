Project C.U.R.E.

The creators of this website recommend this local organization for people living in or near Centennial. Colorado Fourteeners Initiative

This is and opportunity for an environmentalist! The Colorado Fourteeners Initiative is a nonprofit that preserve the natural integrity of the fourteeners through active stewardship and public education. They offer volunteering opportunities, such as trail reconstruction and hiking education!
