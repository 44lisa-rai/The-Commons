Volunteering Opportunities

Denver Max Fund – The Denver Max Fund is an adoption organization that promotes adoption over shopping for pets. They take care of many pets and need volunteers to help with their kind and moving cause. Teens and up can apply to volunteer and make a difference in the wellbeing of adorable souls.

Project Angel Heart – Donate money, decorate food bags, or pack food to help those in need of a meal! Project Angel Heart provides an artistic opportunity to spread kindness and good vibes. Anyone of any age can get involved in this creative initiative.
